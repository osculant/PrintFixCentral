Basic printer setup steps

Regardless of your printer's brand or connection method, the overall setup process involves these three key stages.

Place & prepare

Unpack, plug in power, add paper, and ensure ink/toner is installed.

Connect

Follow the manufacturer's specific connecting method (USB, or Wi-Fi).

Configure

Install necessary software/drivers on your device to enable printing.

Wireless printer setup guide

Connect to your Wi-Fi network and start printing. Place the printer close to the router during setup for best results.

  1. Locate your network's Name (SSID) and Password (usually on the router sticker).
  2. On the printer's control panel, find the Wireless / WLAN setup menu.
  3. Select your home Wi-Fi network from the list.
  4. Enter your Wi-Fi password carefully using the keypad or touchscreen.
  5. Wait for the confirmation message that you're connected.

Important Tip

Make sure your computer or phone is connected to the same Wi-Fi network you want the printer to use. Always refer to your specific printer manual for exact button names.

Printer setup by brand

Simplified guides for popular brands. Exact steps vary by model — always check your user manual for precise instructions.

HP

  1. Select Wireless from the printer control panel.
  2. Choose your Wi-Fi network and enter the password.
  3. Download and open the HP Smart app.
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to add your printer.

Canon

  1. Go to the printer menu and select Easy Wireless Connect.
  2. Open the Canon PRINT app or setup utility.
  3. Follow the steps to detect and connect the printer.
  4. Confirm the Wi-Fi connection on both devices.

Epson

  1. Open the Wi-Fi setup wizard on the printer panel.
  2. Select your network and enter the password.
  3. Download the Epson iPrint app or use the desktop utility.
  4. The software will automatically detect your printer.

Brother

  1. Press the WPS button on your router.
  2. Press the corresponding WPS button on the printer (or use the menu).
  3. Install Brother printer drivers on your computer.
  4. The installation wizard will finish the setup.

Setup on Windows and Mac

Windows 10 / 11

  1. Go to Settings → Bluetooth & devices → Printers & scanners.
  2. Click Add device.
  3. Select your printer when it appears.
  4. If not listed, click “The printer that I want isn’t listed” and follow manual steps.

macOS

  1. Open System Settings → Printers & Scanners.
  2. Click the + button.
  3. Your printer should appear in the list — click it.
  4. macOS will automatically download the correct drivers.

Common setup issues

Printer not detected

Check Wi-Fi connection, restart devices, and reinstall drivers.

Wrong password

Double-check your Wi-Fi password (case-sensitive).

Driver errors

Download the latest drivers directly from the manufacturer’s website.

Frequently asked questions

How long does printer setup normally take?

Usually 15–25 minutes for a standard wireless setup if you have your Wi-Fi password ready.

Can I set up a printer without a computer?

Yes! Most modern printers support full setup using only a smartphone and the manufacturer’s app.

What if I don’t know my Wi-Fi password?

Look for the sticker on your router or log into your router’s settings page (usually 192.168.1.1).
